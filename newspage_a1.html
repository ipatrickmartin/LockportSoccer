The Epic league was a great experience for the kids. It gave them 12 games and brought them together as a team. Playing up two age groups forced us to rely on skill and teamwork, rather than speed and dribbling. Small field games like this really teach the kids to move the ball quickly, think without the ball, and counterattack quickly on turnovers. The championship game was very intense, as they usually are. The kids won 3-1, but it was a battle all the way. It helped focus us for our State Cup playdown games, and we look forward to that league next year.
