The BWNYJSL U15A league brings by far the best competition we have faced all year, without any travel. It is a hidden gem of a league. The other teams are all bigger and faster than we are. If we don't move the ball and play as a team, we suffer. As we learned against Niagara Pioneer. You have to work harder for the goals and bring the ball in deeper, as the goalies are bigger and stronger. You must keep the ball on the ground. You don't win many 50/50 balls against bigger kids. Every game has been a challenge. Hopefully we can stay disciplined enough to do well in this league. Either way it will be a great experience for the team, and is perfect for teaching the kids how to play the game as a team.
